What we catch

Every line entry. Every issue.

Built by someone who did this work manually for years. We flag the things that actually cost you money.

F-01
Block Billing
Multiple tasks bundled into a single time entry, making it impossible to evaluate the reasonableness of each task individually.
F-02
Vague Narratives
Entries like "research" or "correspondence" with no context or matter connection — unbillable under most outside counsel guidelines.
F-03
Above-Market Rates
Hourly rates benchmarked against current market data for your region and matter type. Know immediately when you're overpaying.
F-04
Excessive Time
Routine tasks billed at multiples of what they should take. Form letters, standard motions, and routine calls that clock in unusually high.
F-05
Duplicate Entries
The same work billed more than once across the same invoice or across multiple invoices on the same matter.
F-06
Guideline Violations
Entries that violate your outside counsel guidelines — or standard billing guidelines if you don't have your own in place yet.
